  • 5 counters are invariant across all configs: cnt_h_search=34, cnt_phi_search=14, cnt_decrease=0, cnt_expanded=9, and cnt_generated=14. They reflect the search-flow structure under the INC-symmetric goal-handling, untouched by the three optimization axes. Note: cnt_h_search joined the invariant set on 2026-05-08 when goal-re-push h/Φ calls were re-tagged as PHASE_UPDATE.
  • store_vector wins on cnt_h_update (drives it to 0; cached vector reused for refresh, lazy stale-pop, AND goal re-push).
  • is_opt wins on cnt_phi_update / cnt_h_update (refresh / lazy-pop recompute skipped when responsible goal still active; goal re-pushes still always count).
  • is_lazy=True wins on cnt_push (no bulk re-push); loses on cnt_pop / cnt_pop_stale (stale re-pop counts).
  • Goal-handling is INC-symmetric: at each goal pop, emit on_goal then push (re-push, if non-last). The just-found goal is NOT force-expanded; its successors are reached via other paths during the search. The last active goal is never re-pushed. The goal re-push's h/Φ work is tagged PHASE_UPDATE — it responds to an active-set change, not search progress.
  • No single config wins everything — eager minimizes pops, lazy minimizes pushes; opt+sv layer cheaply on top of either.
