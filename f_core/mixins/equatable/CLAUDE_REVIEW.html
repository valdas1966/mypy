1. Correctness

No change needed.

  1. __eq__ delegates to self.key == other.key with an identity short-circuit. No type guards by design — cross-type comparison raises AttributeError, which is the intended behavior for this internal framework.

2. Readability / Naming

No change needed.

  1. Naming is precise. Class, property, and method names are self-documenting. No unnecessary nesting or indirection.

3. Design (OOP)

No change needed.

  1. Clean mixin pattern: single abstract key property drives __eq__, __lt__ (Comparable), and __hash__ (Hashable).
  2. Identity short-circuit (other is self) is a sound micro-optimization that avoids two key calls.

4. Performance

No change needed.

  1. No type guards — zero overhead per comparison. Only cost is the two key property accesses (or zero with identity short-circuit).
